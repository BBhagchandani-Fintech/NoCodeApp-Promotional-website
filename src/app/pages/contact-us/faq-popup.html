Frequently Asked Questions

What is OfficeBCP?

Ans: OfficeBCP stands for Office Business Continuity Plan. We are here to help you set up your office in the cloud so you can work from anywhere in the world.

Why do I need OfficeBCP?

Ans: Setting up an office and infrastructure is expensive and it is not always possible to be physically present in the office. We will set up your office in such a way that you can then work from the office, beach, bar, coffee shop or anywhere you wish to work from.

What kind of knowledge should / must I have to run/manage this?

Ans: You just need to answer a list of basic questions and we will manage your infrastructure. You don’t need System Administrators once this setup is done. You only need to know how to use a remote desktop and RDP(Remote Desktop Protocol).

What kind of hardware/setup should I have to go with this product?

Ans: All you need is an Internet connection and a device like a laptop or a desktop computer that can connect to your machines in the cloud.

How much time will it take to set up my organization in the cloud?

Ans: A couple of days depending upon the infrastructure needs of your organization.

How am I going to access the machine into the Cloud?

Ans: You can read this user guide. It will help you understand how you can access the machine and work with them.

Which operating system is installed in these machines?

Ans: Windows 10 - Enterprise Edition.

How much money do I need to pay?

Ans: We charge you as per your use and infrastructure setup, on a monthly subscription basis. More information about charges will be available while you are setting up your account.

Is my payment information safe with OfficeBCP?

Ans: For regulation and security reasons we don’t store any critical information with us.

How do you define the machine performance and quality category?

Ans: We have different machine profiles like

  • Light

    1 - Built for general purpose users.
    2 - Suitable for HR-Admin, QA
    3 - Low setup cost

  • Medium

    1 - Built for performance and CPU intensive tasks running once in a while.
    2 - Suitable for users like Developers.
    3 - Medium setup cost

  • Heavy

    1 - Built for performance and CPU intensive tasks running continuously.
    2 - Suitable for users like Designers working on Photoshop and heavy designing tools.
